Hot weather safety
Breed noteSamoyeds run hot. Double coat insulates but traps heat in summer. Kaya needs active management in warm weather.
Normal temp38.0–39.2°C (100.4–102.5°F). Above 39.4°C is elevated.
Quick checkFeel inside her ears with a finger, or between the toes of her paws, for a fast read.
Accurate checkRectal thermometer with lubricant, insert ~2.5cm, hold 60 seconds. Digital pet thermometers are fastest. Ear thermometers are less accurate for dogs.
Warning signsExcessive panting, drooling, bright red tongue/gums, lethargy, stumbling, vomiting. If temp exceeds 40°C, vet immediately.
ProtocolNo walks at 20°C or above. See the Routine page for the full hot-weather protocol and cooling activities.
